About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Technology Dresden in the Software Technology Group. My research interests are:

  • Graph rewriting and bigraphical reactive systems
  • Model-driven software engineering
  • Formal methods
  • Applications of AI
  • Computational models of behavior and emergence
