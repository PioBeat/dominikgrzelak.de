My First Paper and Introduction to AI

Mar 25, 2012

I published my first paper for “Informatiktage 2012” at the B-IT Bonn-Aachen Center for Information Technology, which is a computer science congress organized by the Gesellschaft für Informatik e.V. (GI). I submitted an abstract on the topic of my Bachelor’s thesis and participated in both the poster session and the workshop held from March 23 to March 24, 2012.

It was a great pleasure to meet so many brilliant personalities. Early on, I was introduced to the concept of the Internet of Things and the transformative impact of AI on our future. These experiences have inspired me to deepen my education and explore these exciting areas.
