Joining University of Technology Chemnitz as a Research Associate in MERGE

Mar 15, 2014

I have applied for a part-time position as a Research Associate for MERGE, a Cluster of Excellence at the University of Technology Chemnitz, Germany. MERGE focuses on enabling technologies for multifunctional lightweight structures.

My involvement is specifically within the Interacting Research Domain B, which concentrates on metal-intensive technologies, where I am part of the subproject IRD B4. This subproject is dedicated to evaluate the developed resource-efficient processes for producing hybrid lightweight structures.
