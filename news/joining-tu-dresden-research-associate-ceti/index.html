Joining University of Technology Dresden as a Research Associate in CeTI

May 1, 2019

After the completion of the EU IoSense Project, I applied for a full-time position as a Research Associate at the Centre for Tactile Internet with Human-in-the-Loop (CeTI) – a Cluster of Excellence at the University of Technology Dresden, Germany. CeTI is dedicated to enabling seamless interaction between humans and cyber-physical systems in both real and virtual environments through intelligent communication networks and systems, all in near real-time.

In this role, I will focus on the theoretical building blocks for advancing robotic software by developing context-aware and domain-specific languages that enhance multimodal feedback and streamline programming for specific tasks and environments.
